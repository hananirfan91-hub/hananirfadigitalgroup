❓ Questions about how we work

How fast can you start?

Immediately. Once you approve the quote, we begin within hours – sometimes the same day.

Do I need to pay in advance?

We typically ask for 50% advance for larger projects (websites, branding) and 50% on delivery. For small tasks (thumbnails, image resizing), we may ask full upfront. Always transparent.

What if I don't like the work?

We offer unlimited revisions until you're happy. If we still can't meet your expectations (rare), we'll refund the advance – no questions asked.

Do you sign NDAs?

Absolutely. We're happy to sign a non‑disclosure agreement to protect your idea.

How do I communicate during the project?

Via WhatsApp, email, or call – whatever you prefer. We'll give you direct contact with the person handling your work.
